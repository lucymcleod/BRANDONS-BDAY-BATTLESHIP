Two admirals. Eight ships of sushi. One painfully full stomach.

This is a personalised Battleship game for Brandon's 32nd birthday. Each ship is a sushi piece. When your opponent sinks one of your ships, you have to eat that many pieces of the sushi it represents. A 4-tile California Roll? That's four pieces. A 5-tile Dragon Roll? Better start chewing.

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

  1. Each player opens their own private link — no sharing screens, no cheating.
  2. Place your fleet of sushi-ships in secret.
  3. Take turns firing at coordinates on your opponent's grid.
  4. Hit a ship → the sushi type is revealed to both players.
  5. Sink a ship → opponent must eat that many sushi pieces of that type, IRL.
  6. First to sink the entire fleet wins. Loser pays the sushi tax.
